Who are we?

HackSoc is the University of York's society concerning people interested in computers and computing.

So, will you teach me how to hack?

Yes, but not in the sense that you may think. "Hack" in "HackSoc" means to put together bits of code and make them work.

Then... What do you guys actually do?

Good question. We don't know either.

So, how do I join?

To formally join HackSoc you need to email hacksoc-list+subscribe@yusu.org. You will be regularly updated with what the society is up to and how to help.

Get in touch with us!

If you want to email us you can use hack@yusu.org. We usually hangout on IRC on #cs-york @ irc.freenode.net.
